About Arise

Our mission, our team, and our hackathon journey.

Our Project

Arise is an AI-powered career platform designed for students, created for the PM Internship Scheme Hackathon. Our goal is to bridge the gap between education and employment by providing students with personalized career guidance, skill-building resources, and tools to create a professional identity. We leverage the power of Google's Gemini AI to offer intelligent recommendations and support.

Our Team

We are a passionate team of developers and designers dedicated to empowering the next generation of professionals. This project is a culmination of our skills, creativity, and commitment to making a positive impact.
